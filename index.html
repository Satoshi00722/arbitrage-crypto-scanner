About the app

Arbitrage Crypto Scanner helps traders discover arbitrage opportunities between exchanges and track the best prices in real time. The app scans CEX and DEX venues, accounts for fees, gas and slippage, and helps you act faster without manual monitoring.

Scanning across CEX + DEX on major networks (ETH, BSC, Arbitrum, etc.).
Accounting for fees, gas, slippage and minimal trade size.
Filters by network, exchange, coin, and profit threshold (e.g. > 1%).
Spread & profit alerts (Push/Email/Telegram*).

* Alerts depend on your plan and device permissions.
