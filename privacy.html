Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25.08.2025

This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected by Arbitrage Crypto Scanner, how we use it, and what rights users have.

1. Data We Collect

  • Device technical data (model, OS version, language, IP/country-level location).
  • Diagnostics and crash logs to improve stability.
  • Optional account data for synchronization (email/Telegram ID) if integrations are enabled by the user.

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide and improve app functionality, calculate spreads, and send alerts.
  • Usage analytics in an anonymized form.
  • User support and handling requests.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may only be shared with infrastructure providers (analytics/crash reports/hosting) under agreements ensuring data protection.

4. Storage & Security

Data is stored as long as necessary for the purposes described above, with organizational and technical security measures applied.

5. User Rights

  • Access, correction, and deletion of data.
  • Withdrawal of consent and disabling analytics/alerts.
  • Contact: arbitrage.scanner.help@gmail.com

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Policy, email us at arbitrage.scanner.help@gmail.com.
